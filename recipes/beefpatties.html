Beef Patties

A staple of Jamaican cuisine

Golden, flaky pastries in a semicircle shape containing a spicy ground beef filling. Yum!

Ingredients:

Pastry
  • 450g Plain Flour
  • 227g Butter
  • 1 tbsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 300ml Milk
  • 1 Egg
Beef Filling
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Spring Onions
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • To Taste Ginger
  • To Taste Scotch Bonnet
  • 2 tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 2 tbsp All Purpose Seasoning
  • 2 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 tbsp Black Pepper
  • To Taste Thyme
  • 2 tbsp Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Browning Sauce
